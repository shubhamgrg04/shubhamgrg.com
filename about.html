About Shubham Garg

I am a person driven by the "how" and the "why." Whether I am looking at a line of Python code, a volatile stock market index, or the pressure gauges of an espresso machine, my approach remains the same: a relentless pursuit of optimization, efficiency, and depth.

Based in the vibrant, historic city of Jaipur, India, I balance a modern, data-driven professional life with a rich personal life centered on family, exploration, and the search for the perfect cup of coffee.

The Financial Architect

My relationship with the financial world is not one of speculation, but of rigorous analysis. I have a deep-seated interest in the Indian Stock Market, with a particular focus on the banking sector and the Nifty 50 index.

I don't just "watch" the markets; I deconstruct them—analyzing Net Interest Margins, Asset Quality, and Capital Adequacy Ratios. Beyond broad indices, I've developed niche expertise in the Hospitality and Healthcare sectors, diving into operational metrics like ARPOB and occupancy rates.

The Technical Builder

In the digital realm, I am a builder who values clean execution. My work with Python is a testament to my preference for powerful, versatile tools. I believe in the "indie developer" ethos—finding robust, free-tier deployment solutions that allow for sophisticated automation without unnecessary overhead.

Recent focuses include MCP server deployments, bridging local development with cloud accessibility, and managing web infrastructure through Cloudflare for security and performance.

The Coffee Connoisseur

I don't just drink coffee; I study it. My interest in espresso machines borders on the professional—understanding that great espresso is a variable-heavy equation: grind size, water temperature, pressure, and bean origin all play a role.

The espresso machine is the ultimate intersection of engineering and sensory pleasure—a machine that requires technical mastery to produce a beautiful, artisanal result.

The Strategic Traveler

I approach travel with analytical rigor. From Jaipur, I'm constantly calculating routes to international hubs like Thailand and Japan, leveraging alternative airports and creative flight paths. For me, the journey begins with a spreadsheet and a map.

The Philosophy of Optimization

Finance: Risk-adjusted returns
Tech: Clean code, low-latency deployments
Travel: Cost and experience
Coffee: Flavor profile and texture

I am a lifelong learner who isn't afraid to get my hands dirty in the documentation or the data. I value precision, I cherish my family, and I am always looking for the next "hack" that makes life a little more efficient and a lot more interesting.
